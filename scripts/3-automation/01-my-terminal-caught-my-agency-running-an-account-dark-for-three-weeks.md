## 💼 LINKEDIN CUT
**Hook line:** My agency let an ad account go dark for three weeks. Nobody told me. My terminal did.
**Body:**
I never wanted to be the founder refreshing an ads dashboard at 7am, half-trusting a number someone else typed into a spreadsheet. So I built an agent that audits the account for me, every morning, before I sit down.

It's read-only on purpose. It pulls straight from the source, shapes the numbers, and the credential never enters the model's context. It can see exactly where every dollar goes. It cannot move a single one.

The first time it ran, it didn't congratulate me. It handed me red rows and a stretch of zeros — one account dark for three weeks, zero campaigns running, my best-performing campaign quietly paused. I'd have caught it eventually, on a dashboard, after the budget was already gone. The agent caught it cold.

And it doesn't stop at flagging. The report ends with a section titled "Questions for the agency" — the specific things I should be asking on the next call. I'm not working harder on my ads. I built the thing that audits them, and it has no reason to look away.
