5. **CTA (no website name): click the link below and download 10+ business-automation starter prompts that coach you to full-stack with zero coding experience — first call with Sahil is free.**

**No URL spoken or on-screen** — the avatar always says "click the link below." (You attach your stepaheadco.com/ship link to the post yourself.)

**Keep it honest (brand rule):** the avatar is an actor voicing the product's *real* mechanism (one starter prompt, coach + verify), Sahil's *real* builds, and Sahil's *real* offer (free first consult). Outcomes stay believable — "I built my own working version" — never invented metrics.
