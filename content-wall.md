# content-wall.md — Sahil's Instagram content wall

> The posting plan, not just a pile of scripts. Built on the research into how $50–150 digital products actually sell cold (trust > price; lead with proof; kill the specific objection; sell the identity; warm before you ask for $100). Priority order holds: **PDF → Webinar → Automation → Big-ticket.** Append/re-rank each run.

## The one strategic move that changes the math

Cold reel → $100 checkout is the **hardest** conversion there is. Every comparable creator (Thomas Frank's $90k-in-30-days included) sells the $100 product off a **warm list**, not cold traffic. So the wall isn't "post reels that beg for a sale." It's:

**Proof/teach reel (free value) → capture email with a free first-win → nurture → offer the $100 pack.**

Two decisions for Sahil that the research says are the highest-ROI changes (flagged, not assumed — both need your yes):

| Decision | Why the research says do it | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Free "ship your first thing in an hour" lead magnet** (mini-prompt) as the IG CTA, capturing email | Bridges cold→$100; this is how every $100+ creator actually does it | ⛔ needs your go — do we build a free Module 0? |
| **Money-back guarantee on the $100 pack** | At $100 cold, a refund offer converts "price + risk" → "price only"; close to mandatory | ⛔ needs your go — only then can it go in a script |

Until those are live, the wall drives to **stepaheadco.com/ship** directly and leans hard on the student-wall proof to carry the trust.

## Hook patterns that earn the 3-second hold (use these to re-cut)

- **Result-reveal:** "A beginner shipped [real thing] in [short time]. Here's the prompt." (PDF 05, 06)
- **Student-win:** "My student went from never-coded to [shipped]. Here's what we did." (WEB 02·03, PDF 01)
- **Myth-bust:** "You don't need a bootcamp / a CS degree / to be technical." (PDF 02)
- **Objection-kill:** "Why pay when ChatGPT is free? Honest answer." (PDF 03)
- **Identity:** "You've watched 100 hours and still can't build a blank page." (PDF 04)
- **Guardrail-flex:** "I let an AI see all my ad money. It structurally can't spend a cent." (AUTO 03)
- **Build-in-public:** "I'm building an education brand to 1M dirham with no team." (AUTO 10)
